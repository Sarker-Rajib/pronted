Our journey


Pronted (formerly known as Akshar School Solutions) was started in 2017 with a mission to provide intuitive solutions to learning and management systems for all school needs and make digital education accessible to everyone.

It was the brainchild of Srinivas Rao Narni, an Engineering graduate with over 10 years’ work experience in leading IT companies, based in India and USA. With his proficiency in technology and interest in the education space, he identified the need to mesh technology with learning, and focused on building a strong business model for educational services.
